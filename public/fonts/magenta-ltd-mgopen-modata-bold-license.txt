			<a name="Notes" id="Notes"></a><h2> <span class="mw-headline"> Notes </span></h2>
<p>This is a variant of the Bitstream Vera font license. It has a few minor changes, specifically, the Bitstream Vera license included a note at the end, after
the ALL CAPS section, about the use of certain names in advertising which the MgOpen license does not have, and the MgOpen license includes one additional phrase in the third paragraph:
</p>
<pre>
  &quot;or if the modifications are accepted for inclusion in the Font
  Software itself by the each appointed Administrator.&quot;
</pre>
<p>This license was found at: <a href="http://www.ellak.gr/fonts/mgopen/index.en.html#license" class="external free" title="http://www.ellak.gr/fonts/mgopen/index.en.html#license">http://www.ellak.gr/fonts/mgopen/index.en.html#license</a>
</p>
